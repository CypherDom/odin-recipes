Pork Adobo

Description

Adobo is typically served as a braise in the Philippines, which the Spanish conquered in 1521. The proportions of the ingredients vary depending on your family's preferences as well as your own. Ingredients

  • 500g Pork
  • 1 head Garlic
  • 7 tbsp White Vinegar
  • 1 cup Water
  • 1 tsp Black Peppercorns
  • 2 pcs Peppercorns
  • 1 tbsp Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Combine pork, garlic, vinegar, water, peppercorn and bay leaf in a pot. Cover and simmer for 45 minutes. Strain pork and set aside. (50mins)
  2. Saut√© pork and garlic in oil until light golden brown. Pour cooking liquid and simmer until sauce is thick and coating the pork. (10mins)
  3. Transfer on a serving plate and serve.
